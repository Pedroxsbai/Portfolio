Islamic Mobile Application

Project Overview

BADR is a comprehensive Islamic mobile application created from a meaningful vision: using technology to support Muslims in their daily spiritual life through a respectful, organized, and practical digital experience.

The application was built to make the smartphone a tool for benefit rather than distraction by helping users stay connected to the Quran, prayer, remembrance, supplication, and beneficial Islamic knowledge. The app includes Quran reading and listening, automatic continuation from the last reading position, search, tafsir, verse interaction features such as copy, share, and bookmarks, as well as khatma plans to help users organize and track their reading.

It also offers prayer times, next-prayer countdown, Qibla direction, calendar-related tools, prayer follow-up, last-third-of-the-night calculation, adhkar, duas, fatwas, ruqyah, memorization support, Islamic library features, daily sunnah reminders, zakat calculation, and charity tracking.

What makes this project especially important to me is not only the richness of its features, but the purpose behind it: building something useful, calm, and genuinely beneficial in people’s everyday lives.
